About Us

Venox operates as a trusted subsidiary brand under the umbrella of Velox, a globally recognized leader in smart secu- rity solutions. As a leading advanced ticketing and park management solution, Venox helps park authorities deliver a seamless and secure experience for visitors. With integrated features like RFID-based access control, cashless payment systems, and centralized dashboards, Venox ensures efficient management and robust security. Real-time updates via electronic display boards, employee tracking, and digital locker services, enhance operational transparency, giving park authorities full oversight while improving visitor satisfac- tion. Venox is the trusted solution for creating a well-managed, secure, and visitor-friendly park environment.
